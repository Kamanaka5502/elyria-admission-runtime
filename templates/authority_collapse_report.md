## Bind-Time Authority Requirement

For each consequence-bearing movement, authority must be valid at the time the movement attempts to bind consequence.

Authority is insufficient when it is only:

- implied by role
- assumed by workflow position
- inherited from prior approval
- documented after effect
- detached from evidence
- stale at bind time
- broader than the action being admitted
