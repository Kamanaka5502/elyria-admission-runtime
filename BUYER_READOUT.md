# Buyer Readout

## What this is

Elyria Consequence Twin is a live consequence-admission sandbox that shows whether action can bind before execution becomes operationally real.

## What it proves

This repo demonstrates:

- client-mode protected access
- custom movement intake
- structured evidence attachment
- deterministic consequence verdicts
- signed receipt generation
- receipt replay verification
- current consequence exposure graph
- black-path executive warning
- proof packet export

## Why it matters

Most systems show what happened after the fact.

This repo shows whether a consequence-bearing movement had sufficient authority, standing, evidence, custody, refusal clearance, receipt, and replay basis before it was allowed to bind.

The black path is the executive risk surface. It exposes movement attempting to become operationally real without durable proof.
