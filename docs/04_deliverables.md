# Deliverables

## 1. Consequence Binding Map

A map of consequence-bearing positions and the movements between them.

## 2. Consequence Exposure Graph

A color-coded graph showing admissible, held, refused, and black-path movements.

## 3. Authority Collapse Report

A report identifying where authority is missing, stale, overbroad, inherited improperly, expired, or not replayably tied to the consequence being admitted.

## 4. Evidence Gap Register

A register of missing, insufficient, late, unowned, unverifiable, or non-replayable evidence.

## 5. AI / Workflow Action Exposure Map

A map showing where AI outputs, automation, or workflow actions can influence consequence-bearing decisions.

## 6. Refusal Conditions Matrix

A matrix of conditions under which movement must not bind.

## 7. Revalidation Trigger Map

A map showing which changes force revalidation before consequence continues.

## 8. Executive Consequence Risk Brief

An executive-facing summary of the highest-risk exposure paths, black paths, and required decisions.

## 9. Implementation Blueprint

A build-ready plan for reducing exposure through admission gates, receipts, replay, authority constraints, and runtime enforcement.
